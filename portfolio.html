Python Project: "Space Vanquishers"

We made a moveable spaceship that shoots lazers. You get points for hitting enemies and minus points for hitting satellites or letting enemies touch or pass by you.


Python Project: "Shark Time"

We made a game in which the user controls a shark that moves through a maze avoiding the walls trying to eat fish that are randomly placed within the maze.


Python Project: "Chrome Dino"

Our idea was to make a game like Chrome's T-rex game in which the player controls a little dinosaur that needs to jump over objects coming at him or her at increasing speeds. It didn't end up working properly but was still pretty cool.
